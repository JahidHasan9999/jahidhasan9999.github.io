About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary professional combining startup entrepreneurship with electrical engineering expertise. As a final-year EEE student at North South University, I've successfully bridged the gap between technical engineering knowledge and product innovation.


On the product side, I founded and led Wyseer, an EdTech platform serving university students, and developed an AI-based advising system with 2,000+ organic users. I specialize in system design, UI/UX, and creating user-centric solutions that solve real problems.


On the engineering side, I have hands-on experience in power systems, embedded systems, and control systems. My internship at NSU's Sub Power Station and multiple hardware projects have given me practical expertise in electrical systems, automation, and embedded design using Arduino and STM32.


This unique combination allows me to approach challenges from both a product strategy perspective and a deep technical understanding of how systems actually work.
